About This System

This monitoring system uses Arduino technology with weight sensors to detect potential suicide attempts. When unusual weight is detected on the sensor (such as when someone steps onto a dangerous area), the system immediately:

  • Activates local alarms
  • Sends alerts to designated emergency contacts
  • Provides immediate location information to responders

Warning Signs

Be aware of these common warning signs of suicidal thoughts:

  • Talking about wanting to die or kill oneself
  • Talking about feeling hopeless or having no reason to live
  • Increasing alcohol or drug use
  • Withdrawing from friends, family, and society
  • Displaying extreme mood swings
  • Giving away prized possessions

If you notice these signs in someone, reach out and connect them with help immediately.
